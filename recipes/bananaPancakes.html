Banana Pancakes

Fluffy bananas made from scratch with mashed ripe bananas that are ready in minutes for a delicious twist on ordinary pancakes.

Prep:5 minutes

Cook Time:10 minutes

Total Time:15 minutes

Serving:6

Yield:12 pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoons salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine flour, white sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. Mix together egg, milk, vegetable oil, and bananas in a second bowl. Stir flour mixture into banana mixture; batter will be slightly lumpy.

  3. Step 3

    4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over mdium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

  4. Step 4

    5. Cook until pancakes are golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes per side. Serve hot.

  5. Step 5

    6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutritional Facts

Per serving: 192 calories; total fat 6g; saturated fat 1g; cholesterol 34mg; sodium 282mg; total carbohydrate 29g; dietary fibre 2g; total sugars 9g; protein 5g; vitamin c 4mg; calcium 149mg; iron 1mg; potassium 232mg
