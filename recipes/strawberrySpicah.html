Strawberry Spinach Salad

We love this spinach strawberry salad all year round if we can find straberries! Even the grandchildren love this quick and easy salad.

Prep Time:10 minutes

Total Time:10 minutes

Servings:8

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Toss spinach and strawberries together in a large bowl.

  2. Step 2

    3. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a medium bowl; pour over spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.

Nutritional Facts

Per serving: 235 calories; total fat 16g; saturated fat 2g; sodium 69mg; total carbohydrates 23g; dietary fibre 4g; total sugars 17g; protein 4g; vitamin c 73mg; calcium 135mg; potassium 621mg
